6-Day Curriculum
Day 1
Profiling
Choose your shape, transfer to steel, cut out with zip-cut, refine on belt grinder.
Day 2
Rough Grinding
Establish bevel geometry, set the plunge line, grind to 1mm edge thickness.
Day 3
Heat Treatment
Austenitize at 1465°F, quench in Park50 oil, temper at 400°F. The blade comes alive.
Day 4
Handle Work — Part 1
Prep G10 scales, drill pin holes, mix and apply epoxy, clamp for cure.
Day 5
Handle Work — Part 2
Shape the handle on the belt grinder, sand through grits, hand-finish to smooth.
Day 6
Sharpening
Sharpen your blade to a working edge. Learn technique you can use for life. Take your knife home.
Make-up sessions available ($150/session) if you miss an evening. You don't fall behind — we'll get you caught up.
