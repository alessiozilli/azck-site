Our Story

About AZCK

From Switzerland
to the Peace Country

Alessio Zilli grew up in Switzerland and completed a rigorous 4-year apprenticeship as a blacksmith — earning the title of Journeyman Artistic Blacksmith. The training covered everything from traditional forging techniques to design, metallurgy, and fine art metalwork.

After immigrating to Canada, Alessio settled in Grande Prairie, Alberta, where he founded AZ Custom Knives Ltd. What began as a passion for bladesmithing grew into a full-service operation covering custom knives, artistic blacksmithing, professional sharpening, and hands-on education.

What We Do

Custom Knives

Every blade is handmade — designed for the person who will carry it. From everyday carry to hunting knives, each reflects precision craftsmanship and purpose-driven design.

Artistic Blacksmithing

Hand-forged railings, gates, sculptures, fireplace tools, and architectural hardware. Traditional techniques applied to modern projects with ingenuity and attention to detail.

Professional Sharpening

Knives, scissors, chisels, specialty folders, and restorations. The best professional sharpening service in the Peace Country, trusted by professionals and hobbyists alike.

Education & Classes

Corporate team building, blacksmithing courses, knife making, sheath making, and sharpening workshops. Alessio believes teaching is the highest form of mastery.

The Philosophy

Craft over convenience. Purpose over production. Every piece that leaves the AZCK workshop — whether a knife, a railing, or a sculpture — is built to serve the person who commissioned it. Nothing generic, nothing rushed. The Swiss way.
