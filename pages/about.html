Alessio's Journey

Born and raised in Switzerland, Alessio completed a rigorous 4-year apprenticeship as a Journeyman Artistic Blacksmith under master blacksmiths. That foundation of precision, discipline, and craft shaped everything he does.

He moved to Canada and is now based in Grande Prairie, Alberta. Swiss precision meets Canadian ruggedness. Every blade he makes carries that dual identity — meticulous attention to detail combined with the toughness and authenticity that the Canadian wilderness demands.

At AZCK, we don't make knives for everyone. We make knives for the person who will actually carry them. Custom, thoughtful, and built to last a lifetime.
