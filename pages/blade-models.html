The Bulldog is a palm-sized drop point hunter with flowing curves that make it forgiving on the grinder. It's the model most students start with because the curves grind naturally and the proportions are balanced. Available as a pre-made 1084 blank in group classes or from scratch in 1-on-1 sessions.

Intermediate Difficulty
The Falcon is a tanto-inspired blade with straight lines and a sharp transition at the tip. It's harder to grind than the Bulldog because straight edges are less forgiving on the grinder than curves — any wobble shows. A good step up for students who want a challenge or prefer the tactical look. Available in group classes and 1-on-1 sessions.
