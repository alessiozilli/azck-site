Full Workshop Knife Making

Build Your Own Knife

Shape raw steel into a finished blade in one day, guided by a Swiss-trained Journeyman Artistic Blacksmith. Walk in empty-handed, walk out with a one-of-a-kind knife you made yourself.

Forge & Grind

Heat, shape, and grind the blade. Learn bevel geometry, heat treatment, and the finishing steps that turn steel into an edge.

Handle Work

Select your handle material, shape the scales, and fit them to the tang. The part that makes the knife yours.

Take It Home

Your finished, sharpened knife goes home with you the same day. One-of-a-kind, made by your own hands.
