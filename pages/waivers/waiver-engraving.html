Authorization

Proof Approval

No engraving work will begin until I have reviewed and approved a digital proof. By approving the proof, I confirm that the design, text, spelling, positioning, and orientation are correct and as intended. AZCK is not responsible for errors present in the approved proof.

Positioning Accuracy

Laser positioning is accurate to approximately ±2–3 mm of the approved proof. Minor variations in final placement are inherent to the engraving process and do not constitute a defect or error.

Permanent Process

I acknowledge that laser engraving is a permanent, irreversible process. Once engraving begins, it cannot be undone, corrected, or removed without potentially damaging the item.

I am choosing to have my item engraved at my own risk. I understand this process is permanent and irreversible.

Equipment Risk

I acknowledge that laser engraving equipment, while professionally maintained, may occasionally produce results that differ from the approved proof due to equipment behaviour, material properties, surface inconsistencies, or other factors beyond AZCK's reasonable control.

Liability Limitation

I agree that AZCK's total liability for any engraving error — including equipment malfunction — is strictly limited to the cost of the engraving service (setup fee and per-piece fee). AZCK is not liable for the replacement value, sentimental value, or any other value of the item being engraved, regardless of its worth.

High-Value Items

For items of significant personal or monetary value (including custom knives, collector pieces, heirlooms, and firearms), AZCK strongly recommends testing on a sample piece first. Proceeding with engraving on high-value items is entirely at my own risk.
